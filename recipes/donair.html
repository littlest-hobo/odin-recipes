Halifax Donair

Description

Halifax Donairs are a variation on the classic donair invented by lebanese refugees who moved to Canada due to the Lebanese civil war. The dish is famous for it's spicy meat and sweet, sugary sauce. It is a popular late night snack for people returning home from a night out.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Lamb
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Pita

Steps

  1. Bake seasoned meat for two hours.
  2. Cut Vegetables.
  3. Steam Pita.
  4. Make sauce by mixing 1 part sugar, 1 part condensed milk, and slowly add one teaspoon of white vinegar.
  5. Place meat and Veg on Pita, cover in sauce, roll the wrap tightly.
