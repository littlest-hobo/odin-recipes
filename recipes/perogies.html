Description

Perogies are boiled dumplings, originally from eastern Europe, which have become a popular dish in American and Canada due to the influence of immigrants particularly from the early 20th century. They come in many different varieties, and can be stuffed with anything from fruits to meats and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Potatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Chives
  • Onions
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Arrange dough in small discs.
  2. Boil'em Mash'em Stick'em in the perogies.
  3. Fry meat and onions, adding potatoes at the end.
  4. Add meat and potato mixture to the dough.
  5. Fold the dough into small balls.
