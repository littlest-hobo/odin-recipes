Description

Montreal style smoked meat sandwiches were invented in kosher delis in Montreal. The meat is similar to other dried and smoked meats such as pastrami but takes on a special flavour and texture from it's special spice blend and from the steaming process the meat undergoes.

Ingredients

  • Beef Brisket
  • Spices
  • Mustard
  • Rye Bread

Steps

  1. Salt and spice the brisket. Leave in the the fridge for 8 days, flipping twice a day.
  2. Smoke the meat for 8 hours using maple wood as the smoking agent.
  3. Steam the meat for another 4 to 6 hours.
  4. Assemble the sandwich on rye bread with yellow mustard.
